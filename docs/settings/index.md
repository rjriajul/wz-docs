# Settings

There are two kinds of settings in WZML-X:

- **User Settings** (`/userset`) — your personal preferences. Change your default upload destination, thumbnail, split size, and more. Everyone can use this.
- **Bot Settings** (`/botset`) — bot-wide configuration. Manage variables, upload private files, set categories and drives. Owner and sudo only.

Pick your path:

- [User Settings →](./user)
- [Bot Settings →](./bot)
